About

I've published two books of fiction, Blood and Milk (Sarabande, 1996) and Bloody Mary (Sarabande, 2003).

I'm offering a writing workshop in Greece this summer, for 1 or 2 weeks July 8-25. It's very inexpensive, includes room and board in a tiny town called Kissos on the Aegean coast. Write in the morning, beach in the afternoon... for more info look up Kalikalos.org.
